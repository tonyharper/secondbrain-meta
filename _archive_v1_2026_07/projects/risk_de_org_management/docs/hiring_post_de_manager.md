# Data Engineering Manager - Privacy Areas (Risk pillar)

**Author:** Fernando Bartolo
**Date:** 2025-05-08

- **Level:** M1
- **Location:** NYC
- **Hiring manager:** Fernando Bartolo (Areas DE lead)
- **Other contacts:** Tony Harper (Risk pillar DE lead)

## What Do We Do?

**Privacy Areas Mission:** Optimize how Meta responds to risks to unlock innovation.

## Northstar Success

- **Fast, good risk decisions:** We define standard privacy requirements which accelerate privacy/risk decision making - we respond to regulatory pressure efficiently.
- **Ship "Must Do" work:** We meet our obligations through implementing privacy solutions.
- **Drive Product Velocity & Efficiency:** We improve review & implementation velocity, and ship work efficiently across (time + cost).

## The Opportunity

As a DE Manager on our team, you'll be supporting the NORAM Privacy Areas and driving significant business impact while doing it. You'll have plenty of scope to grow and drive cross-area impact by working closely with partners across Privacy and all the other Product Groups like Monetization, FB, IG, etc.

## Why Join Us?

### High Impact

Privacy continues to be a company priority and the Privacy Areas DE team drives building out core data assets that influence company-wide product strategy across different areas of privacy work.

The team also works on initiatives to improve our understanding of the data warehouse leading to effective and efficient responses to internal and external regulatory pressures.

### Career Development

- Own critical privacy areas with plenty of room for growth and development.
- Work on challenging technical problems that are at the intersection of infra, regulation, and measurement.

### Team Culture

We are a fun and energetic team that values the experience of working well together. We organize get-togethers so the team feels a sense of belonging while working on privacy problems as a team sport.
