# RID Adoption

**Project Tag**: `project:rid_adoption`

## Overview

Driving adoption of RIDs (Re-Identification resistant IDs) as primary identification keys for privacy compliance (Half-Life). By end of H2'24, 95%+ of addressable tables in scope were RIDified. RID STB Linter drove RIDification of new assets to 82% overall, 95% for those with retention > 45 days.
