# H2 Risk DE Dashboard Goals

As I've [shared previously](https://fb.workplace.com/groups/197295836397052/permalink/458302390296394/), dashboards serve as the primary interface between our data and users. Consequently, high-quality dashboards are essential for informed decision-making, and a direct reflection of our DE org.

Our H2 dashboard goals are below. Please note that in H2 we will be onboarding Central Youth onto dashboard standards, and we will evolve our Privacy Dashboard Standards to Risk Dashboard standards.

There are many opportunities for contributing to the tracking and accomplishment of these goals. Please reach out to Steve Fischer if you're interested. Steve will be following up with details on goal tracking and methodology.

## H2 Goals

1. **100% dashboards tagged as Tier 0 or Tier 1 are certified.** During H1 we've allowed some dash certifications to expire. We need to clean these up and maintain full certification going forward.

2. **100% eligible widgets on Tier 0 and 50% of Tier 1 dashboards are built on M360.** Metric 360 will be a major focus in H2, which will take effort from all of our teams. All Risk DEs should make room for this on H2 roadmaps.

3. **95% Continuous Compliance tasks closed within 14 days on Tier 0 dashboards.** This supports dashboard quality beyond just initial certification.

4. **100% D2 queries fixed within 30 days.** This assures our dashboards are responsibly using Meta compute resources.

5. **Ship Goal for Privacy Linter improvements.** This will include fixing coverage gaps, enabling lint checks for M360 and Unified Filters, and evolution to include Central Youth needs.

## A Note on Central Youth

Given that Central Youth is new to the org and new to the dashboard certification journey, we're setting different goals for that team. In H2, we will onboard Central Youth to Privacy Dashboard Standards as we evolve to Risk Dashboard Standards, implementing CY-specific changes as needed.

Starting July 1, **all new CY dashboards** will need to adhere to the current standards. We will migrate existing dashboards to these standards later in H2 and beyond.

## Other H2 Planned Dashboard Work

- Build M360 playbook to assist all teams in meeting our M360 goal.
- Better understand and evolve Risk's use of Unidash AI.
- Run 1-2 rounds of automated no/low usage dashboard archival.
- Convert Privacy dashboard tags to Risk tags.
- Create Unified Filter training for smooth transition to UF.
- Further v2 authoring testing and communication (P1).

Thanks to our DI partners who have helped us formulate these goals, and ensure they can be measured: Adam LaPitz, Arielle Isaacs, Benjamin Rothschild, Brian Schermerhorn, Omri Ziv, Tong Jin.
