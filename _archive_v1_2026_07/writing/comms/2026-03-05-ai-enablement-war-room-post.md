# Risk Data AI Enablement War Room — March 17-19

We're running a focused war room to accelerate AI readiness across Risk analytics. The goal: take a finite set of use cases and deliver end-to-end AI-ready solutions — semantic models, evals, and measurable outcomes — rather than spreading effort thin across the entire domain.

This is Phase 1. What we learn here will shape Phases 2 and 3 in Q2.

## How it works

Participants will be organized into small pods, each focused on a single use case for the duration. Assignment is based on proximity to the problem space. We're keeping pods tight so teams can go deep instead of wide.

## Phase 1 use cases

- **Foundations — Mature Requirements** (MPK)
- **Foundations — Triage & Escalation** (MPK)
- **Areas — User and Restricted Data** (LON) — semantic model for the Risk Area
- **Areas — Transparency & Control** (LON) — semantic model for the Risk Area
- **Privacy Infra — Model Training Compliance** (MPK) — new data foundation for MTC measurement + connection to Risk Review through the AI Risk orchestration layer
- **Youth — Age** (MPK) — semantic model for Age Reconciliation, with e2e authoring and consumption use cases
- **Measurement Framework** (MPK) — comprehensive eval measurement across all use cases

## How we'll measure success

This isn't just "we did something." We're tracking real outcomes: eval pass rates, eval coverage and distribution across use cases, adoption metrics (DAU/WAU/MAU), AI readiness coverage across Tier 0/1 dashboards and M360 metrics, and team-level AI adoption via eWAU@75.
