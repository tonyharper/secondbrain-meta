# Risk DE Org Management

**Project Tag**: `project:risk_de_org_management`

## Overview

Ongoing leadership and management of the Risk Data Engineering organization, including org structure, performance management, hiring, team culture, and strategic communication. Risk DE is the amalgamation of multiple orgs supporting data engineering across Risk (Privacy, Integrity, Security) domains.
