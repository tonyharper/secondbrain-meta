# Dashboard Quality

**Project Tag**: `project:dashboard_quality`

## Overview

Org-wide initiative to raise the bar for Risk DE dashboards. Three pillars: (1) Dashboard Certification as the bare minimum, (2) Canonical Metric Representation via M360, and (3) Designing for Narrative and Usability. Included evolving Privacy Dashboard Standards to Risk Dashboard Standards and onboarding Central Youth.

**Phase**: Completed / transitioned to BAU (archived)
