# Risk Data SEVs

**Project Tag**: `project:risk_data_sevs`

## Overview

Implementation of a Risk Analytics-wide SEV process to improve resilience and quality of Risk data. Includes SEV guidelines for key datasets, SEV reviews, and an IMOC (Incident Manager OnCall) rotation. Three SEV categories: SLO (data lateness), Data Quality (inaccuracy), and Data Inconsistency.

**Phase**: Established — now BAU (archived)
