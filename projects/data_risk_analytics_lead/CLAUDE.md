# Data Risk Analytics Lead

**Project Tag**: `project:data_risk_analytics_lead`

## Overview

Tony is the primary analytics owner and point person for the Data Risk group within Meta's Risk org. He leads a cross-functional team of DEs and DSs responsible for analytics and insights across Data Risk. The role covers coordination, day-to-day decisions, and escalation on the analytics side for all Data Risk areas.

This is currently a cross-functional role — Tony continues to lead Risk Data Engineering while serving as the analytics lead for Data Risk. May evolve into a dedicated team.

## Context

The Risk org restructured in March 2026, moving from the Established Risk pillar to a flatter operating model organized around risk groups. Data Risk and User Risk replaced Established Risk as the two primary groups.

## Risk Areas Covered

- Restricted Data
- User Data Inventory
- Anonymization
- Data Access & Portability
- Data Deletion & Retention
- Anti-Scraping
- Data In / Data Out
- Purpose Limitation
- Cross-Product Data Practices
- Competitive Data Use
- Cross-Promotion
