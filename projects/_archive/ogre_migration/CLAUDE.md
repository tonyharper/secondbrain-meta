# OGRE Migration

**Project Tag**: `project:ogre_migration`

## Overview

Migration of all Risk data pipelines from old Compliance Graph (CG) datasets to new OGRE datasets. SEV S512065 was filed when significant inaccuracies and breakages were identified in old CG data post-OGRE migration. Risk DE went into lockdown in late April 2025 to complete the migration, effectively redesigning/rewriting all data pipelines related to the compliance graph.
