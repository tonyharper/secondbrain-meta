# Risk Review Dashboards

**Project Tag**: `project:risk_review_dashboards`

## Overview

Building and certifying dashboards measuring the new Risk Review (formerly CS 2.0) process. Two primary dashboards launched: Risk Review Health Dashboard (bunnylol: rrhealth) and Risk Review Operational Dashboard (bunnylol: opdash). Dashboards cover all risk reviews created from March 31, 2025 onwards.

## Current Status

**Phase**: Launched and in maintenance (archived)

## Dashboards

- **Risk Review Health** (bunnylol: `rrhealth`) — Analyze reviews reaching Triage, find hot spots and trends
- **Risk Review Operational** (bunnylol: `opdash`) — Analyze efficiency of specific product groups or aspects of Risk Review
