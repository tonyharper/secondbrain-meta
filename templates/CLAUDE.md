# Templates

Scaffolds for new files. Copy, don't over-structure.

- `meeting.md` — skeleton for meetings/YYYY_MM_DD_topic.md
- `project_overview.md` — skeleton for projects/*/overview.md
- `projects_md.md` — skeleton for projects/*/projects.md with checkboxes
- `decision.md` — skeleton for decisions log entry
- `person.md` — skeleton for people/{person}.md
- `doc_ingest.md` — skeleton for inbox/docs/ drop
- `area_overview.md` — skeleton for areas/*/overview.md
